  [![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT_License-green)](http://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)

  ## Contributors
  Guillermo Saenz, Juan Nino, and Carlos Rivas.

  ## Description
  A web-based financial calculator that lets users figure out their capital gains tax, gamble tax, payroll tax, and federal income tax. The goal of this project is to assist users in quickly estimating their tax obligations in relation to their income, tax credits, and deductions. Links to outside resources, such as the IRS, are also provided by the project.
